How to Use

Site Navigation

Use the navigation bar on the side left to choose pages. The next page is also at the bottom of the current page.

For example, the page about the internet will have a link to the page about HTML at the bottom.

Page Navigation

Use the table of contents on the side right to go to sections. Each section is clickable - you can click on them and it will bring you there.

For example, scroll through this whitespace, then click on "How to Use" to get back here.
